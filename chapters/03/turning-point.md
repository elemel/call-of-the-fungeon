This dungeon contains tiles with digits (marked `0` through `9` on the map), swappers (marked `:`), and crossroads (marked `=`). Whenever you step onto a digit tile, push the corresponding number onto the stack. Whenever you step onto a swapper tile, swap the two numbers at the top of the stack.

Whenever you step onto a crossroads tile, pop two arguments from the stack: first `b`, then `a`. If `a` is less than `b`, turn 90 degrees left. If `a` is greater than `b`, turn 90 degrees right. If the arguments are equal, do not turn at all.

After how many ticks do you leave the dungeon?
